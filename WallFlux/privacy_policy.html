Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 21, 2026

1. Information We Collect

WallFlux is a wallpaper browser and automatic wallpaper changer. We do not collect any personal information. All data is stored locally on your device:

  • Saved favorite wallpapers (stored in a local database)
  • Auto-changer preferences (interval, source, search query)
  • Optional Wallhaven API key (stored locally, never transmitted to our servers)
  • Selected local folder URI for local wallpaper source

We do not collect personal information such as name, email, contacts, messages, or location.

2. How We Use Your Information

All locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Display your saved favorite wallpapers
  • Remember your auto-changer configuration between app sessions
  • Authenticate with the Wallhaven API for NSFW content (if you provide an API key)
  • Browse and download wallpapers from the Wallhaven API

3. Third-Party Services

WallFlux uses the Wallhaven API (wallhaven.cc) to search and display wallpapers. When you browse wallpapers, search queries and filter settings are sent to Wallhaven's servers. Please review Wallhaven's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for details on how they handle requests.

WallFlux does not use analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

4. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to our servers. You can clear all app data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data in Android Settings → Apps → WallFlux → Storage → Clear Data
  • Uninstalling the app

5. Permissions

WallFlux requires the following Android permissions:

  • Internet — To browse and download wallpapers from Wallhaven
  • Set Wallpaper — To apply selected wallpapers to your home/lock screen
  • Foreground Service — To run the automatic wallpaper changer in the background
  • Notifications — To show progress when downloading wallpapers in the background
  • Ignore Battery Optimizations — Optional, to ensure reliable scheduled changes
  • Manage External Storage / Read Media — To access your local folder for local wallpaper source

6. Children's Privacy

WallFlux does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13. Adult content (NSFW) requires a valid Wallhaven API key and is disabled by default.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
